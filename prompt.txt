
create a complete, polished 2D side-scrolling platformer game (Mario-style_ as a single self-contained HTML file.

Put the CSS and Javascript in a separate assets folder.

The main character is a black cat. The players play as a black cat named Hexie.

BASELINE REQUIREMENTS
- player runs left/right (arrow keys / WASD) and jumps (spacebar) with smooth gravity and acceleration.
- Solid platforms and ground with reliable collision - the player must never clip through floors or walls
- a camera that smoothly scrolls to follow the player
- at least one enemy you defeat my jumping on it, that hurts you on side contact
- collectible mini pumpkins (instead of coins) with a score counter
- a start screen with intructions
- Smooth 60fps movement

NOW GO ABOVE AND BEYOND - this is the baseline, not the goal. Treat this like a a portfolio piece you are proud of.
- Background music and sound effects, all synthesized with the Web Audio API (jump, collect pumpkin, stomp, hurt, win)
- rich character animation (idle / run/ jump frames)
- particle effects, screen shake and juicy game feel on every hit and jump
- a parallax, layered atmospheric background and a cohesive art style.

The jump should feel satisfying and the whole style should feel alive.

The goal of the game is for Hexie to save his Witch friend who is being held by the bad guys: the Church of Good Manners
